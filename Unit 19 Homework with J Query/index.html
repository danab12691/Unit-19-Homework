My UX Skills

Iteration

Prototyping

The ability to take an idea and draft it and show the intention behind a feature or the overall design concept to users and be able to work and show different aspects of the intended product

Research

Research conducted in person and remotely to dig and dive deeper into the needs, wants, why’s, how’s of a specific User Problem through creating a User Persona, conducting interviews and coming out with an objective
